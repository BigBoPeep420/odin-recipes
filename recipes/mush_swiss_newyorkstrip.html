Mushroom and Swiss New York Strip Steak

Image Credit: Steven Depolo

Description

A juicy, savory New York Strip steak seared to perfection and smothered with sauteed onions and mushrooms and melted swiss cheese.

Pair with your favorite steak sauce, or enjoy all on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 New York Strip Steak
  • 1 Cup Your Favorite Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Yellow Onion
  • 4tbsp Salted Butter
  • 1 slice Swiss Cheese
  • 1tbsp Salt
  • Optional Seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash, dry, and slice your mushrooms. Store in refrigerator.
  2. Peel and slice onion into strips. Store in refrigerator.
  3. Preheat skillet to 400 then add 2tbsp butter.
  4. Once butter is melted, add steak, 1tbsp salt, and optional seasoning. Slowly move steak around skillet in butter.
  5. Monitor sear level. Once to desired sear, add other 2tbsp butter and flip steak. Move steak slowly around skillet in butter.
  6. While searing second side, add mushrooms and onion to skillet. Regularly stir these to keep from burning.
  7. Monitor steak sear level. When completely seared, immediately reduce heat to 275 and flip steak to original side.
  8. Cook steak to desired doneness, flipping as necessary. When nearly finished, add swiss cheese slice to melt on top of steak.
  9. Plate your steak and top with the mushrooms and onions.
  10. Enjoy!
